MEDICAL SCIENCES

Medicine is the science and practice of the diagnosis, treatment and prevention of disease. Medicine encompasses a variety of health care practices evolved to maintain and restore health by the prevention and treatment of illness. Contemporary medicine applies biomedical sciences biomedical research, genetics, and medical technology to diagnose," + treat, and prevent injury and disease, typically through pharmaceuticals or surgery.
